Frequently Asked Questions

What is compound interest?

Compound interest is the process where your investment earns returns, and then those returns also begin earning returns. Over time, this creates accelerating growth.

How does this compound interest calculator work?

This calculator applies a monthly growth rate based on your annual return minus annual fees. It then adds your monthly contribution and projects the portfolio value over time.

Does this calculator include investment fees?

Yes. Annual fees are subtracted from the annual return to estimate a net annual return before compounding.

Is this for loans or investments?

This page is designed for investment growth, not loan repayment. It helps estimate how invested money can compound over time.
