Frequently Asked Questions

What is a savings calculator?

A savings calculator estimates how your savings balance may grow over time based on your starting amount, regular monthly deposits, interest rate, and time horizon.

How does this savings calculator work?

This calculator applies an estimated monthly interest rate to your savings balance and then adds your monthly deposit to project your balance year by year.

Does this savings calculator include inflation?

Yes. It shows both the future savings balance and the estimated value in today's dollars after inflation, so you can better understand your future purchasing power.

Is saving better than investing?

Saving is generally lower risk and more stable, but it usually offers lower returns than long-term investing. Saving can be useful for emergency funds, short-term goals, and reducing risk.

Is this calculator accurate?

It is a simple estimate based on the values you enter. Real savings outcomes can vary due to changes in interest rates, inflation, account terms, and the timing of deposits.
